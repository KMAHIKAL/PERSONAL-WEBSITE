MY PERSONAL HOBBIES

Gunpla Building

Gunpla building is the hobby of assembling and customizing plastic model kits from the Gundam anime series. It involves constructing detailed replicas of robots (called Mobile Suits) using plastic parts that snap together, often with additional painting, weathering, or modifications for a more personalized finish. The hobby encourages creativity, precision, and patience, making it a rewarding pastime for fans of anime, model-making, and mechanical design.

Gaming

Honor of Kings

Honor of Kings is a multiplayer online battle arena video game developed by TiMi Studio Group and published by Tencent Games for iOS, HarmonyOS NEXT, and Android. The game was first released in mainland China on 26 November 2015.

This game is a 5 vs 5 multiplayer moba with a goal to destroy the enemy tower or base to win a match. The match can be divided into 3 part: Early game, Mid game, Late game, each part is 10 minutes. There are 5 main roles or lanes in this game Clash lane, Jungling, Mid lane, Farm lane, and Support.

Subway Surfers

Subway Surfers is a single player mobile gaming app and website developed by Kiloo. The aim of the game is to outrun a train inspector while dodging oncoming trains, barriers and other objects. Collecting coins on the way gives players advantages such as hover boards and high scores.

Block Blast

Block Blast is a puzzle game where players strategically place blocks on a grid to clear rows and columns, aiming to achieve high scores and prevent the grid from filling up.

The game also have an adventure mode, where you complete a task to proceeds to the next level and endless mode where you can try to get the highest highscore by clearing up the blocks. My current highscore is 30k.
